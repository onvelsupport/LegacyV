Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

UK delivery usually takes 1–2 business days after your order has been placed.

Do you offer international shipping?

At the moment, CROWNVII primarily serves customers in the United Kingdom. International shipping may be added later.

Can I return my watch?

Returns are accepted in line with our refund policy, provided the item is unused, undamaged, and returned in its original packaging.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been processed, tracking information will be sent to the email address used at checkout.

Are payments secure?

Yes. Payments are processed securely through Stripe. CROWNVII does not store your card details.

How can I contact support?

You can contact us through the Contact Support page for order enquiries, delivery issues, or product questions.
